About Me

I am Lawrence Lai, born in Australia, raised in Hong Kong, and spent my college and graduate school years in a variety of locations in the United States. I work in the Green Group as a PhD candidate in the Department of Chemical Engineering of MIT, Boston MA.

My role in the Green Group is the chief lab manager, in charge of maintenance of hardware and equipment. My research also employs quantum calculations, database management, and reaction mechanism generation of alkylaromatics to study the behavior of crude oil upgrading and pyrolysis using the group software Reaction Mechanism Generator (RMG) .

As a part of MIT's culture, I was the President of organization Hong Kong Student Society of MIT (Fall 2015 - Spring 2018), in charge of event planning, execution, budget planning, and website development.

In my spare time, I am a martial artist in the art of Taekwondo (rank of 1st Geup Black Stripe) under MIT Sport Taekwondo , competing in the East Coast Taekwondo Collegiate (ECTC) League and the National Collegiate Taekwondo Association (NCTA) . I am also highly involved in video gaming, appreciating both competitive and non-competitive scenes.
